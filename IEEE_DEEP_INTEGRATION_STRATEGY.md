## IEEE Organizational Structure

### What IEEE Actually Is

**IEEE is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit public charity** - the world's largest technical professional organization with:

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Members** | 486,000+ globally |
| **Technical Societies** | 39 specialized societies |
| **Geographic Regions** | 10 global regions |
| **Active Standards** | 1,900+ standards |
| **Tax Status** | 501(c)(3) charitable organization |
| **Founded** | 1963 (roots to 1884) |

The IEEE Standards Association (IEEE SA) is a separately governed body within IEEE that manages standards development, Industry Connections programs, and certification initiatives like CertifAIEd.
